####These are the things that need to be finished for future versions of the game.

**Unfinished. Keep your place in a room or game when you briefly disconnect, instead of being sent back to the lobby and losing your progress. If you drop out, manually or because the server hiccups, your spot is held for a short time, and reconnecting drops you right back where you were, in the same room or game, with your position, wood, and score intact.
**Unfinished. Add a player versus player game mode where players are on both sides of the wall fight: one side attacks and destroys the four walls while the other side builds and repairs them, so players do both the building and the destroying instead of the bots.

**Finished. In an environment versus player game, make the builder bots and the cave's wood sound muffled and reverberant when you hear them from outside the walls, as if the inside of the cave is leaking out through the stone, while your own sounds on the exterior stay clear. The muffle is a low pass filter, and this is the first use of the through the wall audio the player versus player mode will later use on both sides.
**Finished. Give the inside of the cave a natural reverb so sounds down in the cavern reverberate like a real enclosed space, with a soft tail of reflections rather than distinct echoes, while the open-air outdoor areas and the outdoor watcher deck stay dry. It turns on whenever you are inside the cave and off everywhere else, and applies across every game mode.
**Finished. Make the three wood sizes matter when a wall is reinforced, so a larger piece adds more strength than a smaller one, scaled to echo the enemies' weapon tiers: small adds five to ten, medium ten to twenty, and large fifteen to thirty.
