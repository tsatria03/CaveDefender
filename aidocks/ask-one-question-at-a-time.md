When gathering decisions before implementing, ask **one question at a time** and wait for the answer before asking the next. Don't present a numbered list of several open questions in one message.

**Why:** the user reviews by screen reader and prefers to answer sequentially; a batch of questions is hard to work through at once.

**How to apply:** during a planning/confirm step ([[confirm-before-implementing]]), surface the single most important open decision, get the answer, then move to the next. Applies whether asking in prose or via the question tool.
