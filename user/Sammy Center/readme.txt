Sammy senter!
Welcome to sammy senter, by Sam Tupy, collaborating with Mason Armstrong (MTG Studios).
Credits:

Thank you so much Mason for collaborating, helping, and supporting this project. Mason deserves to be credited just as well as I do for his help and support.
What to expect: The sammy senter is a sequel to the badly designed game suite, and it has a lot more content than it. 
Be expecting sidescrollers, grids, and even 3d minigames!
games: 

Avoid the beeps: You have to walk around, making sure no beeping things land on you. You can jump with up arrow to avoid them. Note: The beeps try to follow you, and they seem to get just a little faster after a while, so be carefull!

Cat and dog: Walk around on this 2d grid with the arrow keys, and try to escape the mean nasty dog that is following you, the cat! Try to chase him away for 2 hole minutes, he looks hungry and one hit will kill you. Note: I've only won this game my self one time, so good luck! 

Shoot the target: You have to wait till the target reaches the center, in your stereo field, and shoot it with spacebar before it goes past you. note: After you shoot your gun or when you start the game, you have to load your gun, use control.

car chase: In this game, your in a tipical side scrolling environment with up arrow to jump, and left or right to walk. Go find the gun on this very small bord, and shoot the car before it can hit you! NOte: The car is always going acrossed the screen from left to right then wight to left and so on.

Relax / pause area: This is similar to the one in game suite, but with better sounds, and, it's in 3d! Walk around each level with arrows to walk and space to jump, trying the teleporters, and see what you can do! note: When jumping, hold in the arrow corresponding to the direction you want to go. 

TTS Side scroller: Ever thought your tts voice could pan? well if you thought it could, your right! Type in some text for your enemy to say, and try to kill your tts voice! Notes: 1. Killing your tts voice doesn't actually happen, only in the game, so no worries. 2. the tts voice will do different things each time you restart the game. For example, one time he may stay still and jump like a mainniak, other times he might try to come to you one way or another, sometimes he might run acrossed the screen over and over again, or sometimes he's running away from you! he will even do more than what i've mentioned!

water escape: Fall off the boat, and run away from an insanely fast shark! Just swim with up arrow to the sand and the beach. You have to use all four arrow keys to navigate this 3d grid, spacebar to jump. You have to go to the middle of the beach to win. Hint: The beach starts at y 151 and ends at y 160. The minimum x of the beach is 0, while the maximum x is 250. Hope you can win. 

Guess The Number: Enter the highest amount of numbers you will have to guess. The computer then generates a number between 1 and the highest number. It then pulls up a menu showing a list of numbers from 1 to the highest. Click on one, and if your number is the right one that the computer got, you'll win.

Moneyfall: Fall from 1500 feet off the ground. As you are free-falling, try to collect as many coins as you can. Use the arrows to navigate this 5 by 5 3d grid. Try not to pass up the coins. If you are behind a coin, it wil be  lower pitch. See how many you can get! Press h to check your health, s to check your current score, and c to check your coordinates. Spacebar does an interesting thing. It makes you go up instead of down. When this is pressed, you will go up as many feet as the amount of coins you have. And, the price for cheeting in this way, Your health gos down by 3 times How ever many feet you go up! Your health will go up by one every foot you fall, and up by 15 if you get a coin.

Baloon popper: Pop as many of those dang things as you can! a 1d side scroller. 1 and 2 select your different weapons. fists has a small range but gives you more points, and knife has a longer range and makes it easier but gives you less points. Press up arrow to jump, baloon's can't hit you when your in the air. 

Lightning power: a 1d sidescroller. get to the middle of the house, and kill it. the middle is at x 10. You can hit it easier. but when your to the left or right of it, if you strike, by the way, with spacebar, you hit it lighter. and then if your not at ten, you don't die, but the house ganes health if it crashes. but if you brave it and go to ten and fire your lightning, it will hit hard. good luck! OH, and, if your at ten and the house crashes, you go... crunch! 

Wallbreaker: Oh gosh, how did you manage to get your self into 1, this situation, and 2, this room. With enemies on the other side of the reinforced wood walls, banging non stop until the wall falls down. But there's a sort of problem. When there banging, it's raddling the roof so hard that wood is litterily falling from the roof. Navigate this 2d grid with your 4 arrow keys. And remember, north is up, south is down, west is left, and east is right. If you step over a piece of wood you pick it up. But that can be time consuming, so you can just press space when your with in a 5 square range of the wood. Trust me, this will be helpfull. Press s to hear the wall strengths, w to check your wood, and c to check your coordinates. Once you pick up wood, run over to the desired wall, and press enter to put a piece of wood there and increase it's strength. How long can you survive? we'll have to see. won't we. 

Santa save: Can't santa just deliver gifts normally? with out any problems? maybe? maybe not? Well, I hate to say it, but it's all up to you. There is this big fat unnamed beast who wants nothing more than to destroy that, thing, over on the left hand side of the sidescrolling map. And that thing, is the slay, with all the gifts. And that beast is mad. Attempt to keep up with him as he runs to the left hand side. Keep wacking him with your knife you have, by pressing the spacebar. make sure he doesn't make it over to the slay! Every time you kill him your level goes up. But if he kills you, or if he goes over and well, um, makes some short work of that, thing, then it's game over. How long can you survive, how high level can you get to, and who can get the highest score! You can press e for level, h for health, and b for the health of the beast. Jumping also works too, and when you jump, he can't hurt you. also, a hint, try pressing the left and right arrow instead of holding them!

Exit the fun way: Want to exit the sammy senter? Why not have some fun in the process! Run around with the arrow keys, and try to find the beep. Step on it and the game will exit. Note: every 9 seconds, your position will go to x 0 y 0, so move quick!

Utilities: 

Minitimer: Need to keep track of time? Well, heres a small sulution. Start the timer by clicking on it in the main menu, and your off! 
You can even tell how far into a minute you are by listening to the panning of the click sounds! It will make a sound when a minute is up, and chime after an hour is up. 
Press e to see how much time has elapsed. Press space to pause and unpause the timer. If you want to know some debug, press m to mod the time! Have fun making it go to -150,000 hours! also, pres enter to stop the timer and have the time be coppied to your clipboard in hours, minutes, and seconds.

Score Viewer: Showing as "view Scores", Score viewer allows you to view past scores in games that have them. It first shows the games list in the scores folder. Click on the game you want. Then, it shows the dates. Click on the date you want. Then, it shows the times and then after that the scores for that game at that time.

Text utilities: Just a whole bunch of fun text utilities to play around with and/or use.

Other fun:
Piano: Yes, there was a small, and buggy piano in the game suite. Well, this one is a lot better! Now you have ` threw = for piano keys. 

Recording with the piano! Yes, yes! Now, you can press control + r to record your piano pieces! Press control + s to stop. Press control + p to play exactly what you've recorded!

Saving recordings: Isn't that nice? You can save your recordings to show friends! Just press control + f2!

Loading recordings: To load a recording/piano track, just hit control + l. A menu will pop up, and you can select a track. Tracks are located in the recording folder witch wil be created when ever the game needs it. 
When you've selected your track, just press control p to play it as normal. It will act like you've just recorded that piece and your ready to play it!

other: 

tts options: This dialog will appear when you have to select tts settings. You can use up and down errows to select a voice. You can use left and right arrows to select rate, page up and down for volume, and home and end for pitch. Notes: rate, volume and pitch only work if the screen reader is not the default / highlighted option. press enter to save your changes / go back or what ever is supposed to happen in what ever case your in. In any case however, that will exit the menu and continue other execution.
