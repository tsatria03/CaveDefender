Player help.

This is the list of commands every player can use, no matter their rank. Press slash to open the chat, type a command, and press enter to send it. Words in angle brackets are things you fill in yourself; don't type the brackets.

/who: Lists everyone who is currently on the server.
/uptime, or /serveruptime: Tells you how long the server has been running.
/getmotd: Reads out the server's message of the day.
/staff: Lists every staff member, online or offline, along with their rank.
/pm <player> <message>: Sends a private message to a single player.
/help <player or staff>: Opens the player help, or the staff help, in a readable window. For example, /help player or /help staff.
